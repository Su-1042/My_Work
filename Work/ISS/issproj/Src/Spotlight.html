Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift is an American singer-songwriter who rose to fame in the mid-2000s with her country-pop music.

She has won numerous awards, including 11 Grammy Awards.

In addition to her music career, Swift has also acted in films and television shows.

She is an influential figure in popular culture and has a massive fan following around the world.
