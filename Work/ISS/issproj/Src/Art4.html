Shawn Mendes

Shawn Mendes is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and musician.

In 2015, he released his debut album, "Handwritten," which included the hit single "Stitches."

Mendes has released several successful albums and singles, including "Treat You Better," "There's Nothing Holdin' Me Back."

He has won numerous awards, including several People's Choice Awards and MTV Europe Music Awards.

Mendes is known for his soulful voice and heartfelt lyrics.
