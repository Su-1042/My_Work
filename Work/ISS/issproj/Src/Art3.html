Linkin Park

Linkin Park was an American rock band that formed in 1996.

The band achieved worldwide success with their unique sound.

Some of their most popular songs include "Numb," "In the End," and "Crawling."

Linkin Park's music and impact on the music industry continues to be celebrated by fans around the world.
